--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
■ sxdefectalign
(https://sxrepo.mpie.de/)
(https://sxrepo.mpie.de/projects/sphinx-add-ons/files)

□ Install
0. sudo apt install grace
1. wget https://sxrepo.mpie.de/attachments/download/20/sxdefectalign.bz2
2. bunzip2 sxdefectalign.bz2
3. chmod +x sxdefectalign

□ Manual
1. wget https://sxrepo.mpie.de/attachments/download/36/sxdefectalign-manual.pdf
2. evince sxdefectalign-manual.pdf

□ Example: quantum espresso (GaAs, Ga vacancy, 3-)
1. gedit inp
---------
&INPUTPP
  prefix=’GaAs’
  outdir=’relax_pris’
  filplot=’relax_pris.pp’
  plot_num = 11
/
&PLOT
  nfile=1
  filepp(1)=’relax_pris.pp’
  iflag=3
  output_format=6
  fileout=’v_elec_pristine.cube’
/
---------
2. ~/qe-6.5/bin/pp.x < inp
3. ~/sxdefectalign -q 3 --eps 12.9 --vdef v_elec_defect.cube --vref v_elec_pristine.cube --ecut 30 --qe --average 5.2
  (you can get dielectric constant 12.9 on ph.x)
4. xmgrace -nxy vline-eV-a0.dat
  xmgrace -nxy vline-eV-a1.dat
  xmgrace -nxy vline-eV-a2.dat
  (you estimate a plateau value of C=-0.01eV for the green line.)
5. ~/sxdefectalign -q 3 --eps 12.9 --vdef v_elec_defect.cube --vref v_elec_pristine.cube --ecut 30 --qe --average 5.2 -C -0.01

□ step7: defect formation energy
1. defect formation enegy = Etot(GaAs_VGa(3-)) - Etot(GaAs) +  Etot(Ga)/4+ q*(EVBM + EF) + Defect correction (eV)
  = (-132.86315351 - (-138.47843590) +  -18.01711232/4)*13.6058 + -3*(EVBM + EF) + 3.1661 [eV]
  = 18.2822 -3*(EVBM + EF) [eV]
   (Ref [10] case: EVBM =  3.9923 eV (for undefected supercell))
  = 18.2822 -3*3.9923 -3*EF = 6.3053 -3*EF [eV]
※ Note: Fermi energy EF = electron chemical potential μe at 0 K
2. Ga-rich (or As-poor) limit: 6.3053 -3*EF [eV]
  As-rich (or Ga-poor) limit: 6.3053 -3*EF + ΔH [eV] = 6.3053 -3*EF + -3.8898/2 = 4.3604 -3*EF  [eV]
※ 2GaAs(c) = 2Ga(c) + As2(g); ΔH°(298.15 K) = 89.7±1.2 [kcal/mol] = 3.8898±0.05204 [eV]
  https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/0021961474902559
  計算で形成エネルギーを計算すると下記のようになる。
  As-rich (or Ga-poor) limit: 6.3053 -3*EF + ΔHf(GaAs) [eV] = 6.3053 -3*EF + -0.9970 = 5.3083 -3*EF  [eV]
※ Here we briefly discuss the other ingredients needed to calculate defect formation energies using the Zhang-Northrup formula. First, the valence band position ?v must be obtained from a calculation on the pristine unit cell, with a dense enough k-point sampling so that the band edge is included (for GaAs this just means that the Γ point is included). [9] (where ?v is the valence band maximum, then ?v = EVBM)

□ infromation
----------
ε(ω) = εelec(ω) + εlat(ω) + εdisp(ω)
ε(ω) ≒ εelec(ω) + εlat(ω): semi-conducter or Metal oxide case

εelec(ω) (ω < 10 PHz): all materials
  Polarization of the electronic state of matter caused by an electric field
  10 PHz: Vacuum ultraviolet region
εlat(ω) (ω < 10 THz): Compound liquid and solid
  It is caused by a change in the arrangement of the atoms that make up the compound due to an electric field.
εdisp(ω) (ω < 10 MHz ? 1 GHz): Molecular liquids and polymers with dipoles
  Caused by the orientation of a molecule or polymer dipole with respect to the electric field

ε(ω=0) = ε1(ω=0)
ε(ω≠0) = ε1(ω≠0) + i*ε2(ω≠0)

static permittivity εs = limω→0 ε(ω)
Ref: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Permittivity

ε(ω) = ε∞ + (εs - ε∞)/(1-iωτ)
Ref: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dielectric

The dielectric tensor can be split in the ionic (ε0) and electronic (ε∞) contributions:
  εij = εij0 + εij∞
The refractive index, n
  n = (εpoly∞)0.5
where εpoly∞ is the average of the eigenvalues of electronic contribution to the dielectric tensor. It should be noted this equation for the refractive index assumes the material is non-magnetic.
Ref: https://wiki.materialsproject.org/Dielectric_calculations
----------

※ 計算手順を確認するためにこのページを作成したので、実際には、カットオフを30 Ry、3x3x3のスーパーセル、k点数を増やす、収束条件を厳しくするなどして計算精度を上げて進めて欲しい。K_POINTS automaticでの最後の3つの数字を0にするとΓ点を含めたk点が設定される（出力ファイルにどのようにk点が設定されているかが表示されている）。
